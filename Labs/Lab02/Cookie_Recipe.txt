Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened to room temp.)
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1 egg (room temp.)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 and 1/2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
1 cup dark chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line two baking sheets with silicone baking sheets or with parchment paper.
2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt.
3. In a separate mixing bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar for 1-2 minutes until throughly combined. Mix in the egg and vanilla extract. Slowly add the dry to wet ingredients until well combined. Mix in the oats, and then the chocolate chips without using the mixer.
4. Cover tightly and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
5. Scoop the dough into little balls and leave room next to adjacent cookies.
6. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10-13 minutes, or until the tops of the cookies are set.
7. Remove from the oven and cool on a baking sheet for 5-10 minutes, then enjoy!

These are the best cookies ever.
